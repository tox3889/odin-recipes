Chinese Dog Meals

Description

This recipe is for chinese dog breeds, a popular one being pugs. The dish is called 'Rice with Meat and Veggies', a classic. And yes, chinese dogs can eat rice. They evolved for this, after all.
The aroma of the steamed jasmine rice combined with the tender, voluptuous chicken breast...🤤

Equipment

  • Mao (right dog)
  • John the Baptist (left dog)
  • China Plate
  • Pan
  • Proprietary blend of cooking utensils

Ingredients

  • Jasmine rice
  • Whatever veggies you want
  • Any meat but dog
